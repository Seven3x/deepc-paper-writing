# Contributions

1. A minimal warm-start / transfer mechanism that prevents the degraded DeePC bank from staying frozen in the same data state as the nominal bank after fault onset.
2. A controlled quadrotor benchmark showing that the transferred degraded bank improves average fault-aware performance over the old nominal/degraded scaffold under single-rotor efficiency degradation.
3. A conservative experimental boundary analysis showing where the gain holds and where it remains incomplete, especially relative to MPC and across the mild cases.
